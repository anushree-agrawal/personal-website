A little bit about me...

I'm a rising sophomore at Yale University and an incoming Engineering Practicum Intern at Google in Seattle, WA. I enjoy working on products that people use on a daily basis and I'm interested in using technology to make new advances in healthcare technology. I've worked at Intel Corporation in Santa Clara as an Internet of Things Intern, focusing on smart healthcare devices. I've also been involved in bioinformatics and wet lab biology research at the University of North Texas and MD Anderson Cancer Center.


At Yale, I'm a Teaching Assistant for CS50, our introductory programming class. I'm also part of the organizing team for YHack, Yale's 36-hour collegiate hackathon. I help run events for Yale Float, our women in computer science club and I also like to take an art class every now and then.
